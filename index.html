About

Taskify

Taskify is a web to-do list platform designed to help you manage your daily tasks in an easier and more organized way. With a simple yet functional interface, Taskify makes it easy to log, track, and complete tasks, increasing your productivity and helping you achieve your goals more efficiently. This platform was created out of my own need for a tool that could effectively manage my tasks and streamline my daily activities, ensuring that nothing important gets overlooked. Whether it's for work, study, or personal projects, Taskify is here to keep you on track and focused on what matters most.

Features

Add Task

Easily add tasks to your to-do list by providing a title and description. Stay organized and ensure you never forget any important task.

Delete Task

Remove completed or unnecessary tasks with a simple click. Keep your to-do list clean and focused on what matters most.
